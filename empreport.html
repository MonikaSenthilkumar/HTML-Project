Employee Details

An employee database should include basic personal information such as Name,Employee id,Department id,Salary. Each employee is a vital part of the team, bringing unique skills and experiences. Most employees hold degrees in their respective fields, ranging from Bachelor's degrees in Business Administration, Engineering, and Computer Science, to Master's degrees in specialized areas such as Finance, Marketing, and Human Resources. An employment detail is the employee's information pertaining to their employment. You can view information of employee such as their employment details and the assets provided to them by the organization.
